Introduction of Facilitating Language and Literacy Development

Infants along with toddlers experience maximum growth in language proficiency from environments that provide ample opportunities for speaking and listening in addition to conversing tasks which promote language abilities and literacy abilities. Listening sessions that include reading and meaningful conversations combined with developmental experiences of early reading and dialogic learning build future literacy abilities (Berk & Meyers, 2023; Whitehurst et al., 2021)
